ABOUT

RN CABLES Cables are reputed pvc insulated cables manufacturers from Ghaziabad, U.P. PVC stands for polyvinyl chloride. It is extensively used in electrical cable production for isolation, bedding and wrapping. Earlier, rubber insulated cables were used for general household wiring. But, now PVC has replaced the rubber cables due to long service life, safety and cost-effectiveness. It is considered to be one of the most versatile cable constructions due to its properties, including easily modified with plasticizer, lubricants, stabilizers and other ingredients. In addition to others, it is also easy to recycle and process in the place of common thermo plastic type.


RN CABLES is among highly reputable PVC insulated cables manufacturers. We manufacture a huge assortment for PVC flexible wire, twisted cables that can be used in varied industries or common household that include electronic wire, round flexible wires, instrumentation computer cables, T.V. wires, hook up wires, submersible cables and so on.
